Our Story

Resort-Level Craftsmanship & Care

Founded in Malibu, Swell Clean was built on a simple vision: property care should mirror the refreshing, pristine nature of the ocean itself.

We grew up on these beaches, watching how the relentless salt mist and humidity weather the beautiful architectural designs along the coastline. We set out to create a premium pressure washing service that matches the standards of five-star luxury resorts.

By combining high-tech commercial gear with state-of-the-art soft washing solutions, we ensure that every deck, driveway, tile roof, and siding panels we spray looks brand new, preserving your home's integrity against marine erosion.
